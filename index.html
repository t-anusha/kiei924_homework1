Kellogg Business of Healthcare Conference 2018

Is Tech Delivering on its Healthcare Promise?

January 20th, 2018

Theme

In recent years, the healthcare industry has invested billions in tech. Are these investments delivering on the promise of lower-cost, higher-quality care and a better patient experience? Where are companies experiencing success, and what are the pitfalls they should consider?

Join us on January 20, 2018 at the Kellogg Global Hub in Evanston as the 18th Annual Business of Healthcare Conference addresses some of these questions.

Panels

Beyond the office

Is technology expanding access for under-served rural or low-income populations? How are companies think about designing solutions for these communities? More broadly, what digital health tools have actually been successful in empowering patients while minimizing noise for physicians?

Making data more productive

Limited access to integrated data has made it difficult so far for advanced analytics to make an impact in healthcare. How do we overcome these barriers to make health data more productive? What are providers, payers, and EHR vendors doing to address these challenges?
